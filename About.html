  • MUSIC

    • The Histrionics is a band from the void within Louisville, Kentucky composed of Quinton Byrd, Rylan Lamb, and Phoenix Hardy. Their music has been described as "Jim Morrison rising from the grave to create a dark, bluesy avant-grunge sound" (Scott Recker, The Leo)
    The band's music was created first and foremost as an attempt to tell surreal stories using conceptual lyricism and songwriting, telling stories of the aberrant, the grotesque, and the unloved.
